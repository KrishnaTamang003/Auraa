Frequently Asked Questions

Do you offer delivery across Nepal?

Yes! We deliver to all major cities in Nepal including Kathmandu, Pokhara, Lalitpur, and Bhaktapur. Delivery typically takes 2-5 business days within the Kathmandu Valley.

Can I visit your atelier?

We love visitors! Our studio is open by appointment only. Email us at hello@auraatelier.np to schedule a private tour and experience our craft firsthand.

Do you offer wholesale or bulk orders?

Absolutely. We work with boutique hotels, spas, and specialty retailers across Nepal. Contact wholesale@auraatelier.np for pricing and minimum order requirements.

Can I create a custom scent or design?

Yes! We offer bespoke creations for special occasions, corporate gifts, or personal collections. Minimum order of 25 pieces. Reach out to discuss your vision.
